Camper Van Build

Design and Fabrication
Project Overview
COVID-19 hit during my senior year of highschool, and by the time I was about to go to college it looked like my entire freshman year would be taken from my bedroom. In the face of this I decided to take a gap year, buy and old cargo van and convert it into a camper. I spend the rest of the year traveling in my camper van Charlie.
I insulated the van and installed a floor, walls, and ceiling, build a kitchen cabinet/counter, a bed, and an overhead storage compartment, and wired up a 12V electrical system charged by Charlie's alternator and solar panels to power lights, a fridge, an inverter, and some other applianced.
